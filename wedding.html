That Fateful Night

September 22, 2012; Columbia University
In an effort to make her house party for the Orchestra Club a little bit more appealing, my college roommate (and now bridesmaid!) Dana decided to extend the invitation to friends & other people we knew. Because yes, you're right, an Orchestra party doesn't sound all that poppin'. (Dana who built this website, is greatly offended by this sentence) So call it what you want - serendipity, fate, or a Colorado guy on the hunt for some smart ladies - but Mike ended up at that party in my college apartment. He had on a GIANT flannel shirt, some crusty Nikes, and hair to his shoulders. But despite all that, he was tall, super handsome, and brought his own beer... a man after my own heart.
